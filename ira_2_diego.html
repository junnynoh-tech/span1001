Cultural Spotlight — España

Connected to Vistas Panorama: Chapter 2
🗺 Panorama: España

University Life in Spain

Spanish universities operate on a very different schedule than American ones. Diego's day-to-day life reflects some of those cultural differences.

🗺 Geography
Madrid is Spain's capital and largest city. The Universidad Complutense, where Diego studies, is one of the oldest universities in the world, founded in 1293.
🍽 Food culture
Lunch (el almuerzo) is the biggest meal in Spain, eaten between 2–4 PM. Dinner is usually after 9 PM — very different from American meal times.
💬 Language variation
In Spain, vosotros is used for "you all" — Diego might say ¿cuántas clases tenéis? In Latin America, everyone uses ustedes instead.
🎓 Personal connection
Spanish students often stay at home during university (rather than living on campus). Diego commutes to his university every day from his family's apartment.

📝 Grammar Reminder: estar + location

Use estar (not ser) to say where someone or something is.

  • Estoy en la clase — I'm in class
  • Estoy en la biblioteca — I'm in the library
  • Estoy en el trabajo — I'm at work
  • Estoy en casa — I'm at home
