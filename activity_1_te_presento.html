Part 1 — Preséntate

Record a 30–60 second video introducing yourself in Spanish
📌 How to Access Bongo Open this assignment in D2L and scroll to the bottom. Click the Bongo video link to start recording.

Your video must include all three of the following:

  1. 1A greeting and your name — e.g., Hola, me llamo…
  2. 2Where you are from — use Soy de…
  3. 3Your title or occupation — choose one from the Word Bank below
Example script

Hola. Me llamo Ana.

Soy de Georgia.

Soy estudiante de español.

Gracias.

💡 Tip Use your real information or invent a persona — be creative! The goal is fluent, natural-sounding Spanish.

📝 Grammar Note: Using de in Spanish

Think of de as "of" — it links a noun to a field of study or a place.

  • Field of study: estudiante de matemáticas  ·  clase de español
  • Place / organization: mesero de Starbucks  ·  miembro de un club

Part 2 — Respond to a Classmate

Watch two classmates' videos and introduce one to the other
📌 Before You Begin In Bongo, watch at least two classmates' Part 1 videos. Choose one and record a video comment.
  1. 1Greet the classmate by name — Hola, Carlos…
  2. 2Introduce a third student to them — Te presento a [name].
  3. 3Say where that person is from — Es de…
  4. 4State their occupation or major — Es estudiante de…
  5. 5Share two similarities using Ustedes son… and Son de…
Example response

Hola, Carlos.

Te presento a Ana.

Es de Georgia. Es estudiante de español.

Ustedes son estudiantes.

Son de Georgia.

¡Chau!
